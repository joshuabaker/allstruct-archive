Tufnell Park

Rear extension, 2017

This project is the refurbishment and reconfiguration of the ground floor and side-return of a beautiful 4-storey family home. Our work included the installation of a rear and side extension with huge new skylights infusing the north-facing lower ground with natural light. We installed floor to ceiling crittal windows with stained glass panelling to create views onto the garden and to frame an incredible Magnolia tree.

We also created a new open plan kitchen and living space on the ground floor linked with a bespoke stair box and brass handrail, laid reclaimed oak flooring throughout and added bespoke joinery including a floor to ceiling library feature in the ground floor reception room.

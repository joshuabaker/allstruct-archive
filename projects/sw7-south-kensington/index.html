South Kensington

Apartment, 2019

This 18-week project was the modernisation of a 2-bed apartment in South Kensington. The property was fully stripped with floors pulled up, enabling our team to re-wire and re-plumb the apartment to create two new bathrooms, three generous bedrooms and a new kitchen. The project was completed with bespoke joinery and stunning oak patterned flooring throughout.

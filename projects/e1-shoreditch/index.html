Shoreditch

Listed terraced house, 2015

This project involved substantial works to revive a Grade II listed, 4-storey Townhouse in East London. The house features a new stone staircase which we installed on top of the existing concrete stair in the basement, as well as bespoke fitted joinery throughout, including a large marble top kitchen on the ground floor, floating sink units and a limestone steam room in the upper bathroom.

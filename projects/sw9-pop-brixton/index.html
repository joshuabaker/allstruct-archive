POP Brixton

Culture & Events, 2015

POP Brixton is an event showcasing exciting new brands and businesses in an eclectic collection of multicoloured Boxtainers.

Allstruct created the fit out here for The New Zealand Cellar – a London store selling the best of New Zealand's small production wines and boutique vineyards. We fitted windows and glass doors to the structure and two rooms for tasting, with wall to wall shelving created out of original wooden casing.

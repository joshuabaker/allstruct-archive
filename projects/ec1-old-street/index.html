Old Street

Warehouse conversion, 2015

Our work here renovated an existing Victorian warehouse building to create a stylish and modern home. As a building with an eclectic history as an engine house, sawmill and workshop – all associated with the Whitbread Brewery – retaining the original integrity and character was essential.

Exposed brick walls, original oak flooring and beams were retained and our joinery work used complementary materials such as leather, reclaimed limestone from Heathrow Terminal 2 and dark woods for a finish in keeping with the history of the building.

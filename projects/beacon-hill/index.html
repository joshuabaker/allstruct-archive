Camden

Garden extension, 2017

Allstruct completed this innovative rear extension at a North London home, comprising a new kitchen, larder and wc. The roof was clad in zinc and we supplied and installed a large Lonsdale rooflight to flood the kitchen with natural light. Oak chevron flooring was laid to match the rest of the property and carefully laid popham tiles incorporated. Bespoke joinery creates a modern bespoke finish while a large zinc-clad 'pop-out' window seat provides a relaxing reading area for the family.
